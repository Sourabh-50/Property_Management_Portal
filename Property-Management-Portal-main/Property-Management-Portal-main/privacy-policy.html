Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact details
  • Property information and documents
  • Government identification numbers
  • Payment information
  • Device data and usage information when you use our service
  • Survey data and user feedback
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • IP address and session data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process property registrations and transactions
  • Verify your identity and documents
  • Communicate important updates
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Anti-breach and system for safeguard data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls and authorizations
  • Secure data storage and backup

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request data corrections
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of communications
  • File a complaint
